THE ART AND THE HEART OF JOURNALING

Journaling is an art. The first step is to find a small free and secure space where you can ease your mind and fin security in yourself. I personally journal everywhere I go. From making it your main source of relief, to simply writing my thoughts away when I feel an intense mood.
